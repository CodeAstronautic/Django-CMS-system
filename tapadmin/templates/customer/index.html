All About US

‘Construction PLUS’ is a website In which we can get online help of the designers, engineers and Vasthu experts for the construction of our new houses or buildings etc... In this site we will get the list of contractors, engineers and hardware dealers in our locality. There is a form for entering our ideas or concepts like three bed rooms and facilities which we want for our home or our offices and we will get an estimate to our plan from differentEngineers. A user can register in this site and if he is a premium user he can view and download different plans for the house or similar constructions. We get the plan and estimate through email. We can apply for the hard copy also. There is an opportunity to contact the desired engineers as per our budget.
